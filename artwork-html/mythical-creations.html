神秘宝石造物

2024 / Painting

Software: ComfyUI

Size: 200cm x 200cm

Concept

"The Realm of Autumn Treasure: Mythical Creations of the Ages" is an exhibition inspired by mythology and fused with technological innovation, creating a space rich in visual impact and cultural depth. "The Realm" symbolizes a mysterious domain that transcends everyday experiences, while "Autumn Treasures" signifies the maturity and harvest of life, reflecting the cycles and sustainability of nature and human culture.

As a testing ground for GenAI technology, the exhibition's works reshape the understanding and interpretation of traditional mythological symbols. Through the new forms generated by GenAI, symbols traditionally rooted in history and cultural heritage are detached from their original imagery and reinterpreted into a modern visual language. This transformation endows them with fluidity, dynamism, and regenerative qualities, turning them into cultural symbols with a renewed vitality.
